Notes / Assumptions
  • TPC and MCT1cm are interpolated from the hydrostatic table at the solved mean draft. If table is absent, fixed fallbacks are used.
  • LCF and LCB are interpolated from the table. LCG/LCF/LCB reference is midship (+ forward).
  • Density correction: with table present, displacement is solved directly using W/ρ; without table, ΔTmean = (W/TPC) × (ρref/ρ).
  • Trim sign convention: stern trim (+), bow trim (−).
